Project

Forma

Category

Brand Identity

Year

2024

Client

Forma Interiors, Copenhagen

A furniture brand built on restraint and tactility.

Forma needed an identity that could hold stillness without becoming inert. The visual system draws from Scandinavian object-making traditions — plain forms with exact edges, materials that age into character.

Typography was set in a single weight across all surfaces. Color was restricted to warm stone and unbleached cotton.

The result is a brand that feels made rather than designed — present in the room, never competing with the furniture itself.
