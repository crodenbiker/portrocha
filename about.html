Felipe Rocha
Founder & Creative Director

Felipe Rocha is a designer and creative director with over 18 years of experience shaping brands at the intersection of business, identity, and culture. His work spans global commercial and cultural contexts, partnering with companies and institutions to build brands that resonate at scale. Prior to co-founding PORTO ROCHA, he held roles at Spotify and Sagmeister & Walsh, and worked at Fabrica in Italy on COLORS magazine. He is a member of the Alliance Graphique Internationale.

images/founder_leo.jpg
Leo Porto
Founder & Creative Director

Leo Porto is a NY-based Brazilian creative director and co-founder of PORTO ROCHA. His work centers around brand identity systems, building robust frameworks that are both precise and expressive — designed to scale and evolve with culture. Prior to founding the studio, Leo was a Design Director at COLLINS and worked at Pentagram, Mother, and Chermayeff & Geismar & Haviv. He has taught typography at the School of Visual Arts in New York, and has been recognized by D&AD, The One Club, Print Magazine, and The Type Directors Club.
